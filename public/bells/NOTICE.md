# Audio attribution

The files `bell-strikes-1.wav` through `bell-strikes-8.wav` are sourced from the
"Bells / Gongs" pack by Benboncan on Freesound:
https://freesound.org/people/Benboncan/packs/4023/

`bell-strikes-16.wav` (used once a year, at New Year's midnight) is a derivative
work made by concatenating `bell-strikes-8.wav` with itself, with a short pause
in between - "eight bells for the old year, eight bells for the new."
